An Invitation

Most women don't lose themselves all at once.

It happens slowly. A relationship ends. Your body changes. Life gets busy, heavy, complicated. Somewhere along the way you stop recognizing the woman in the mirror — not because she disappeared, but because the story you've been living inside stopped being true.

At some point there's a quiet moment where something inside you says: "This isn't me anymore."

Witnessed was created for that moment. This is an intimate experience for 10 women who are ready to see themselves differently — through photography, storytelling, time in nature, and the presence of other women walking their own turning points.

The story you've been carrying begins to loosen its grip. And something unexpected happens — you start seeing yourself differently. Not by an audience, not by the world, but by yourself.

Your Guide

Stephanie Shanks

Photographer · Retreat Guide · Former Massage Therapist & Energy Worker

Witnessed was born from my own turning point.

Several years ago my life cracked open. After a divorce, health struggles, and losing the community I thought would always be there, I found myself barely recognizing the woman I had become. I felt disconnected from my body, ashamed of where my life had landed, and unsure how to find my way back to myself.

One day driving home from a doctor's appointment, something inside me drew a very clear line: this isn't me.

What followed wasn't a dramatic reinvention. It was a slow return — walking every day, healing my body, getting sober, spending long hours in nature, rebuilding one small decision at a time. Photography became part of that return. I realized that being truly seen can be one of the most powerful catalysts for change.

Before becoming a photographer I spent ten years as a massage therapist and energy healer, holding space for people during vulnerable moments of release and transformation. Witnessed brings those worlds together: healing, storytelling, nature, and photography as a mirror for the truth of who you are.

My role isn't to change you. It's to create a space safe enough for you to see yourself clearly again.

Questions

Everything you
want to know

Do I need photography experience?

Not at all. This is not a photography class. You just need a camera or a phone and a willingness to turn it inward. If you've never picked up a camera in your life, you're welcome here.

What kind of camera do I need?

Any camera works — including your iPhone. The intention behind the shot matters infinitely more than the gear. We'll even teach you how to see differently regardless of what's in your hand.

Is food provided?

Breakfast and snacks are provided on Saturday morning. You'll have time to grab lunch or dinner on your own — there are great spots nearby, and we build in plenty of time for it.

Can I commute both days?

Yes! The day rate gets you full access to all sessions, the hike, and everything else. Many women prefer to have their own space at night — completely valid.

How many women will be there?

Maximum 10. This is fully intentional. You will know everyone's name. You will feel held. You will not get lost in a crowd.

What should I bring?

Camera or phone, comfortable layers for an early morning hike, your journal if you like to write, and an open heart. A complete packing list is sent upon registration.

Is there a payment plan?

Reach out — let's talk. We never want money to be the reason someone doesn't show up for herself.

What is your refund policy?

Spots are transferable up to 2 weeks before the retreat. Refunds available up to 30 days prior to the event. Reach out and we'll work something out.
